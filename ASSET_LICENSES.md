# WindowSeat V2 media licences

WindowSeat V2 does not hotlink production media and does not include third-party railway footage, music, announcements, logos, social-media downloads, stock previews, or unverified images.

## Generated route plates

The six source landscape plates were generated specifically for this repository with OpenAI image generation on 15 July 2026. They were reviewed for watermarks, text, prominent people, malformed infrastructure, and obvious geographic impossibilities before inclusion. Each source was resized, optimised, cropped, and lightly colour graded into two AVIF scene derivatives and one WebP poster.

The routes are presented as **inspired by** their named regions. The generated imagery is not a claim of documentary or location-level geographic accuracy.

## Audio

The running train, cabin, wind, and rain beds are synthesised locally at runtime with the Web Audio API. No sound recordings or copyrighted music are bundled.

## Interface assets

Icons and the favicon are original inline SVG/CSS work created for this project. No official Indian Railways logos or official affiliation are used or implied.
